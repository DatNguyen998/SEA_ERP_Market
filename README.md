# 🌏 Southeast Asia (SEA) ERP Market Research

Hello and welcome to this repository. This space is dedicated to my ongoing research and notes regarding the Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) market across Southeast Asia. 

---

### 👤 A Bit About the Author
I am **Kent (Nguyen Thanh Dat)**, a Business Analyst based in Vietnam. My daily work revolves around understanding how systems like SAP and Odoo can solve real business problems. Because I am deeply curious about how technology impacts our region, I created this project to quietly study the broader landscape of ERP adoption, demand, and growth in Southeast Asia. 

---

### 🌱 The Purpose of This Project
The primary goal of this repository is **learning and market observation**. 
As a Business Analyst, I believe that building good systems requires not just technical skills, but a deep understanding of the market's pulse. Through this research, I hope to answer questions like:
* What are the unique challenges SEA businesses face when adopting ERPs?
* How is the demand shifting between enterprise solutions (like SAP/Oracle) and agile, SME-friendly platforms (like Odoo)?
* What role does regional culture and infrastructure play in digital transformation?

This is a personal sandbox for organizing data, insights, and industry trends that I gather along my professional journey.

---

### 🗂️ What You Will Find Here
As I continue to learn and compile data, this repository will gradually include:
* **Market Overviews:** Summaries of ERP demand in key SEA countries (Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, Singapore, etc.).
* **Industry Trends:** Notes on cloud adoption, automation, and AI integration within regional ERPs.
* **Data Analysis:** Small analytical projects or visualizations (using tools like Python or SQL) to map out market share and growth metrics.
* **Case Studies:** Brief looks into successful (and unsuccessful) ERP implementations in the local market.
